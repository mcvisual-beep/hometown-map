Local College Student Spots Lexington, KY

About this

This map shows some of the different spots on and near UK that I tend to frequent when I am on campus. These locations are close to one another so that a person could potentially walk from one point to another in less than 30 minutes. The point data was taken from Google Maps and polished in GeoJson.io.
